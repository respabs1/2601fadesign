Why Mathlearner?

Mathlearner is created by olympiad students, for olympiad students. We're not a massive research organization—we're passionate learners who want to share what we've discovered through our own journey in mathematics competitions. Our goal is simple: make learning mathematics more enjoyable and effective by sharing the strategies, tricks, and knowledge we've gained from years of olympiad preparation.

Through Mathlearner, we've built a platform where students can learn the same problem-solving techniques and mathematical insights that helped us succeed in competitions. We believe the best teachers are those who have walked the same path. By offering bite-sized lessons, interactive problems, and explanations based on real olympiad experience, we help students understand mathematics the way we learned it—through practice, curiosity, and breakthrough moments. Mathlearner is our way of giving back to the mathematical community and helping other students achieve their goals.

How to Use the Application

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email and create a personalized profile. Set your learning goals and preferred difficulty level to get started on your mathematical journey.

2

Begin Your Learning Path

Choose from various mathematical topics and start with foundational lessons. Each lesson builds upon previous knowledge, creating a structured learning experience.

3

Complete Challenges & Level Up

Solve problems, complete daily challenges, and earn XP points. Just like in Duolingo, you progress through levels, unlock achievements, and build an unbreakable learning streak.

4

Track Your Progress

Monitor your statistics, view detailed analytics, and see how you're improving. Compete with friends on leaderboards and celebrate your milestones.

5

Master Advanced Topics

As you level up, unlock advanced mathematical topics. Access olympiad-level problems, live tutoring sessions, and exclusive resources from experienced mathematicians.

How Mathlearner Was Created

Our Story

Mathlearner started as a simple idea: two olympiad students wanted to help others learn the way they did. We realized that many students struggle with mathematics because they don't have access to experienced problem-solvers who can break down complex concepts. We decided to create a platform where we could share our knowledge and experience directly.

Building the Platform

We built Mathlearner using our understanding of what makes learning effective. Rather than fancy algorithms, we focused on clear explanations, real olympiad problems, and interactive challenges. We made sure every lesson reflects the way we learned—through practice, repetition, and gradually increasing difficulty. The platform is designed to feel like having a knowledgeable friend guide you through mathematics.

Content From Experience

Every problem, explanation, and tip in Mathlearner comes from our personal olympiad experience. We've handpicked problems from actual competitions we've participated in, and written explanations based on how we understand mathematics. This is authentic knowledge shared by students who have been exactly where you are.

Community-Driven Learning

Mathlearner is meant to be a community of learners. We're continuously improving based on feedback from students using the platform. This isn't a finished product—it's a growing resource created by and for the mathematics community. Your success is our success.
